## 💡 About me
I'm a student majoring in Digital Communications and Multimedia Engineering at HUST and an active researcher at **ANSA Lab**. My core strengths lie in **deep mathematical logic, abstract thinking, and high-efficiency multitasking**. I approach system design with a rigorous, analytical mindset, often leveraging high-level mathematics, probability, and physics proofs to break down and solve complex technical bottlenecks. 🧠

My ultimate academic and professional trajectory is geared toward **Advanced DevOps and Cloud Architecture**. I am currently building a strong research profile to pursue a Master's degree in South Korea, targeting top-tier institutions like KAIST, Yonsei, and SKKU. 🌍

🔭 **Currently working on:**
- Researching and deploying Cloud Computing virtualization infrastructure (OpenStack, Docker, Kubernetes).
- Developing and optimizing the "Cloud System Management Simulator" using C and Linux.
- Building robust system monitoring architectures using Prometheus, Grafana, and Telegraf.
- Enhancing system analytical skills through complex mathematical modeling, Bayesian statistics, and kinematics.
